Game For Peace Gameplay Modes

Mode Description
Classic Mode
  • Maps: Island, Rainforest, Snow, Desert
  • Gameplay: Supports first/third person, 100 players, single/double/four-person teams, parachute to search for supplies, fight as the safe zone shrinks, and survive to win.
Entertainment Mode
  • Speedy Battlefield: No airdrops, high supply refresh rate
  • Fully Armed: All items and guns are refreshed, with abundant airdrops and supplies
Creative Workshop Mode
  • Super Body Confrontation: Eight super body professions, use skills and strategies to eliminate opponents and finally win
  • Special Operations: Can return to the field, with different arms and skills
  • Extreme Cold Mode: Snow map, need to collect fire materials before the blizzard
  • Firepower Duel: Includes recall gameplay, with heavy firepower weapons and helicopters
  • Point Occupation Competition: 4-person team competes for strongholds, and the first to occupy two strongholds wins
  • Dark Night Crisis: 60-person game, resist the tide of corpses and players, with exclusive props
Team Competition Mode
  • Warehouse Mode: 4v4 team up, unlimited resurrection, the first to kill within the time limit wins
Special Training Island Mode
  • Upgraded version of the training ground, including shooting training, fun gameplay, and interactive facilities

There are so many ways to play Game For Peace. No matter what type of player you are, you can find a mode that suits you! If you like to play exciting games, you should play the classic mode. 100 people "fight" on a super large map, pick up equipment, run to the safe zone, and the one who survives in the end "wins the chicken dinner".

If you want to experience freshness, you may play the creative workshop mode. The recall mode can revive teammates, and the dark night crisis mode lets you fight against zombies. The entertainment mode focuses on relaxation and joy. The Jedi sniper mode allows you to become a sharpshooter. There is so much fun in this game!

There is also a team competition mode suitable for teaming up with good brothers to "play black". The special training island mode is like a super large game park. Practice shooting and play small games. There is always one that can touch your heart!
