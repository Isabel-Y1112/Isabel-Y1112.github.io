Minecraft: Where Imagination Comes to Life

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang Studios. It was first released in 2011 and has since gained a massive global following. In Minecraft, players explore a blocky, procedurally - generated 3D world with virtually infinite terrain and can discover and extract raw materials, craft tools and items, and build structures, earthworks, and simple machines. The game's open - ended nature allows players to create and experience the game in their own unique ways.
