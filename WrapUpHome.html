Game For Peace: Where the thrilling battles take place

Game for Peace (和平精英), also known as Peacekeeper Elite is an official PUBG game made specifically for and only available in China. It is developed by LightSpeed & Quantum Studios and Tencent Mobile while is very similar to PUBG MOBILE but having more weapons and less game modes. It is much more well known in China and is one of the most popular games in Mainland China. Even having a social media platform made for the game: 和平营地. (Sources: Wikipedia, PUBG Fandom)

I have been playing this game for many years. The reason I like this game is that I really enjoy shooting and the short-term excitement, since they bring me great joy and make my heart beat faster. I want to make more people see this game, experience the thrill, and play with their friends.
